About Saket Gyanpeeth

"NURTURING POTENTIAL"

Delivering quality education for over 29+ years

Saket Gyanpeeth was established in the year 1994 with "NURTURING POTENTIAL" as its motto. The main purpose of the trust is to render selfless service to the society through value-based education. The noble concept of service to humanity: service to God has truly inspired our founder Chairman Dr. Ram Ujagar Tiwari, under whose guidance Saket Gyanpeeth is promoting better cause of education in Kalyan, Maharashtra and other remote areas as well.

The Trust provides medical facilities to needy and deserving persons by organizing camps from time to time and encourages all-round development of the future generation by giving numerous scholarships and free ships to brilliant and needy students.

2017

Ph.D. Research Center - PRUTSIM

Received approval for Ph.D. Research Center in Management, And Affiliated By University Of Mumbai.

Pramod Ram Ujagar Tiwari Saket Institute of Management is a well-established center of excellence in management education, strategically located in Kalyan East, Thane District.

With a legacy spanning over a decade and a half, the institute has consistently evolved as a dynamic hub for academic rigor and industry-oriented learning. It is recognized for its progressive pedagogy, blending traditional academic values with contemporary business practices. The institute emphasizes holistic development through experiential learning, case-based teaching, and industry interface. Its robust curriculum is designed to align with emerging market trends and corporate expectations. The faculty comprises experienced academicians and industry professionals who foster critical thinking and leadership skills. The campus provides a vibrant ecosystem that nurtures innovation, entrepreneurship, and ethical business practices. Students are groomed through structured mentorship, skill development programs, and real-world exposure. The institute takes pride in its strong placement track record with students securing opportunities in reputed corporate organizations. Graduates have successfully been placed in leading companies across diverse sectors, reflecting the institute’s commitment to employability.

The Ph.D. Research Center at the institute serves as a catalyst for advanced academic inquiry and knowledge creation. It promotes high-quality research in management and allied disciplines, encouraging scholars to address real-world business challenges. The center provides a supportive research environment with access to academic resources, expert guidance, and interdisciplinary collaboration. Through its research initiatives, the institute strengthens its vision of becoming a knowledge-driven and innovation-focused institution.
